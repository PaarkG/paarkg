I'm currently studying CS at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, considering a BS/MS in 4 years or a Robotics Engineering double major. In highschool I was the primary programmer for my robotics team for 3 years, and it definitely inspired me to pursue a career in what I call real programming, which is ironic for me to say while programming a website, since I always considered web design to be my last choice for career.
